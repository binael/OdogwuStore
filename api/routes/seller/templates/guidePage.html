Seller Guidelines

Welcome to our platform! We're excited to have you as a seller. Follow these guidelines to get started:

Step 1: Register as a Seller

Visit our registration page and create a seller account. Provide the required information about your business and products.

Step 2: Complete your Seller Profile

Enhance your seller profile by adding detailed information about your business, logo, and a brief description. This helps build trust with customers.

Step 3: Add Products to Your Store

Use your seller dashboard to add products to your store. Include clear product descriptions, high-quality images, and accurate pricing information.

Step 4: Set Up Payment Methods

Configure your preferred payment methods in the seller settings. This ensures smooth transactions with customers.

Step 5: Fulfill Orders Promptly

Monitor your orders regularly and ensure timely order fulfillment. Provide shipping updates and communicate with customers when needed.
